THE CASTLE

The Castle is a tone-poem I wrote in early 2019, inspired by depictions of Dracula's Castle (especially in art from the Castlevania series of video games). I also wanted to experiment with the instruments available in the EastWest Symphonic Orchestra VST set, which was gifted to me in 2018.

I actually started working on this in the summer of 2018, but I ran into a pretty big case of writers block. I eventually managed to finish it, taking some inspiration in the middle sections from the chord progressions of Marty O'Donnell.
